FAQ
How much does a fence cost per linear foot?
It depends on material, height, gates, and site conditions. This tool returns a low/avg/high range to plan around.
Do gates increase the total cost?
Yes—gates add hardware and labor. This calculator adds a per-gate allowance.
Does removing an old fence cost extra?
Often yes. Removal and disposal is usually a separate line item.

This standard layout is built for service providers, photographers, and local brands that need more than a simple one-pager—without going full custom.

Layout highlights

Clear sections for services, story, social proof, and FAQs. Western details show up in typography and accents, but the overall feel stays clean and modern.

Multi-page Service focused Photo-friendly

Core sections in this demo

  • Hero & intro: clear tagline, quick description, and primary call-to-action.
  • Services grid: 3–6 services, each with short descriptions and “starting at” prices.
  • About section: your story, why you do what you do, and what makes you different.
  • FAQ & details: answer common questions so clients feel confident before reaching out.

On your real site, each section would be built from your actual copy, photos, and colors. This demo exists to show structure, spacing, and how the western-meets-modern style can look in practice.
